Your CSV can use any column names. After uploading, Claude will analyze your data structure and propose a mapping. You review and confirm before the dashboard generates.

The only requirement is that your dataset contains both self-reported and objective measures (e.g. estimated vs actual screen time), a participant identifier, and a time/day variable.
